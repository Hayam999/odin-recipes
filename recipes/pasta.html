Pasta

Description

Pasta is a versatile and beloved staple in many cuisines, especially Italian. Made primarily from wheat flour and water, it comes in a variety of shapes and sizes, from long strands like spaghetti to short tubes like penne.

Pasta can be served in numerous ways, from simple preparations with olive oil and garlic to elaborate dishes with rich sauces, meats, and vegetables. Its ability to absorb and complement a wide range of flavors makes it a popular choice for meals around the world, enjoyed for its comforting and satisfying qualities.

Ingredients

  • Flour: This homemade pasta recipe starts with two cups of all-purpose flour.
  • Eggs: Eggs lend fat and moisture to the pasta dough.
  • Oil: Two tablespoons of olive oil add moisture and help the dough come together.
  • Salt: A teaspoon of salt takes the flavor up a notch.
  • Water: Add just enough water to form a smooth, thick dough.

Steps

  1. Make the dough.
  2. Turn the dough out and knead it for 10 minutes.
  3. Let the dough rest.
  4. Divide the dough into balls.
  5. Use a pasta machine to roll and cut the dough into desired shapes.
